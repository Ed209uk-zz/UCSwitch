Your responsibilities

When you claim Universal Credit there are a number of activities you become responsible for in order to receive your payment. You can get more information about Universal Credit online at www.gov.uk/universalcredit

What you can do to prepare

Activities you can do now to help your switch to Universal Credit;

  • You will need a bank account that you can make payments, from as youâ€™ll need to pay your own rent etc. If you have a partner you may want to consider a joint account.
  • You will need to provide identification for your new claim. You could do this now by registering online with GOV.UK Verify. If you can verify yourself through this website you may not need to attend a Jobcentre to bring in your identification.
  • You will need an email account so you could set one of these up now.
  • You could use the online budgeting support tool to help plan for moving to monthly payments.

If you require extra support in applying for Universal Credit this will be available in Jobcentre Plus offices and other local support organisations.
